It Is My PASSION To Create custom 5e dice set and DnD accessories. I love how enthused other players get when they have dice made to fit their character and I made Thunder Moon Dice to help bring this custom aspect to their gameplay. All of my dice are individually tested to make sure they are balanced and are within weight and size tolerances for official standards.

All products are custom made. Any product you order will be made for you individually and may not match the images of products shown.

DICE - Thundermoondice was founded on the desire to create custom, personal connections to characters and the collective experience of players at the table. Dice are only made available for purchase after being rigorously tested for balance regardless of the inclusions used. Additionaly the resin used to create them is non-toxic once cured.
POTIONS - Tokens and bottles! We have simple, easy to read Tokens relating to 5e healing potions, displaying the value of healing from the humble Common up to the luxurious Supreme. If you might prefer to roll the dice as well, we have Potion Bottles as well - shake them and pour out your potion to see just how Supreme it was! The dice contained and resin used meet the same standards of care and service of all of our dice.
